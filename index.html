About Me

Hi! I'm Sinura Damsath Perera, a passionate young web developer and tech enthusiast from Sri Lanka, currently studying at Ananda College, Colombo. I enjoy turning ideas into creative, functional, and user-friendly digital experiences.

I have a strong interest in web design, front-end development, and creative branding. I work with technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and design tools such as Canva and Photoshop to build modern websites with clean layouts and smooth animations.

Beyond coding, I love exploring AI tools, digital design, and problem-solving, and I'm always eager to learn new technologies and improve my skills. I've worked on several school and personal projects, including presentations, web apps, and brand concepts, which have helped me develop both technical and leadership skills.

My goal is to grow as a full-stack developer in the future and create impactful digital solutions that help people and businesses. I believe in continuous learning, creativity, and building things that truly make a difference.
